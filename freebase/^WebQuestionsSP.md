# WebQuestionsSP

**WebQuestionsSP**<sup>[[1]](#myfootnote1)</sup> is the annotated version of WebQuestions, which contains questions that require a 1 or 2-hop relation path to arrive at the answer entity. More specifically, about 40% of the questions require a 2-hop relation to reach the answer. Freebase is its backgound KB. Originally, it splits into 3,298 questions as train set and 1,639 questions as test set.
